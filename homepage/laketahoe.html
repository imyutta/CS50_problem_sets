Lake Tahoe! ❄

Lake Tahoe is best in winter because it is filled with exciting events, gorgeous weather, and enough winter activities to keep the whole crew happy. If you like powder skiing and snowboarding, ice-skating and sledding with the kids, and all things snow, a Lake Tahoe winter trip is your ticket to paradise!
