HALF MOON BAY!

Half Moon Bay boasts the best of Northern California's San Francisco Bay Area in all in one place. With a vast and diverse landscape that encompasses miles of wide sandy beaches, lush blufftop meadows, and rolling forested hills, there is plenty to explore.

Our enviable coastal setting—with a dynamic working harbor and thriving surf and watersport culture—will inspire you to hop aboard a fishing boat, kayak, or stand-up paddle board, and explore our diverse marine ecosystem.

Half Moon Bay's historic Main Street boasts over one hundred small, locally-owned businesses, including unique boutique shopping, mouthwatering eateries, stunning art galleries, and plenty of free parking in easy walking distance of it all.
